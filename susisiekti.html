dry steam cleaners

Dry steam cleaners are cleaning devices whose steam does not contain any liquid water droplets. This is achieved by a very high temperature and high pressure, which makes the steam dry. This is why we speak of dry steam. With the use of a little water and electricity and no cleaning agents, all our dry steam devices achieve what even conventional steam cleaners often fail to do: residue-free cleaned and germ-free surfaces.
